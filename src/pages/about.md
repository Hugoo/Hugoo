## 👗 The Dematerialised

I was in charge of designing and building the initial version of [The Dematerialised](https://thedematerialised.com/) in 2021. The NFT marketplace hosted multiple drops with famous brands such as RTFKT, The Fabricant or Vogue Talents. It grew from zero to thousands of users and more than half a million euros of revenue.

- [Luxury Brands Are Already Making Millions in the Metaverse](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2021-12-09/luxury-fashion-brands-are-already-making-millions-in-the-metaverse)
